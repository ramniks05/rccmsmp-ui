info Guidelines

If you are a new user, please first click on "Registration" to create your account, then proceed to "Login". If you already have an account, please follow the instructions below:

Mobile Number Login:

  1. Select "Citizen" or "Operator" as your login type.
  2. Choose "Mobile Number" login method.
  3. Enter your 10-digit Mobile Number in the Mobile No. field.
  4. Click the "Send OTP" button to receive OTP on your mobile number.
  5. Enter the 6-digit OTP received on your mobile number.
  6. Enter the CAPTCHA code displayed in the image.
  7. Click the "Sign in" button to access your account.

Mobile/Email & Password Login:

  1. Select "Citizen" or "Operator" as your login type.
  2. Choose "Mobile/Email & Password" login method.
  3. Enter your Mobile Number or Email in the username field.
  4. Enter your Password in the password field.
  5. Enter the CAPTCHA code displayed in the image.
  6. Click the "Sign in" button to access your account.
