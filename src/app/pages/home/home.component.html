Revenue Court Case Management System

About the System

The Revenue Court Case Management System (RCCMS) is an online platform designed to streamline and digitize the management of revenue court cases in Manipur. This comprehensive system enables citizens to track their cases, access documents, submit applications, and stay updated on case proceedings. The system provides transparency, efficiency, and easy access to case-related information for both citizens and court operators, making the entire process more accessible and user-friendly.
